Questhub.io

Questhub is a public to-do list with social features and game points.

Concepts

Social Todo List

Questhub allows you to announce your plans, gather the feedback, and find other people to cooperate with on common goals.

Tasks, gamified

Gamification is great for tuning people motivations in a positive way.
Questhub gives you points and achievements for each task you complete.

Collaborative prioritization

By liking other people's quests, you promise them points, — or give them points immediately if they already completed the quest.
So they can quickly gather opinions on what the community would like to be see more — new feature, internals refactoring, or new blog post.

FAQ

"Quest"? "Player"? What is this?
Quest is the any kind of task you're planning to do. Player is you, and other people are players too. You get 1+{number-of-likes} points for each quest you close.
There are many other bug trackers and to-do lists! How is Questhub different?
Questhub considers people to be more important than projects, and fun to be more important than planning. We're going to optimize its mechanics for:
  1. People feeling appreciated;
  2. Praise being granted and received wherever and whenever necessary;
  3. Contribution simplified as much as possible.
There are many more ideas for future features, check out our DesignDraft.
Is Play Perl realm for Perl 5 or for Perl 6?
Both.
And also Moe, p2 and anything else that will grow out of Perl community in the future.
Can I use this for my non-Perl tasks?
Yes! Just add them in the Chaotic realm.
This doesn't motivate me.
That's ok. People are different.
I can't use this without feature X.
Create a quest on Meta hub and unclaim it. I'll pick it up if I like your idea.
Alternatively, report it on Github.
Or, maybe, why don't you implement it yourself?
How can I add a link to a CPAN module in comments easily?
If your Perl module name contains ::, and you're on Play Perl realm, it'll turn into the link to MetaCPAN automatically. Use cpan:Foo syntax for one-word module names.

Technology

Questhub is built with:
  • Perl Dancer
  • Backbone.js
  • Bootstrap
  • MongoDB

Backend is completely separated from the frontend. Backend API is documented here, feel free to use it.
(But please don't abuse game mechanics or you'll be banned.)

Questhub sources are open. Clone it on GitHub!
